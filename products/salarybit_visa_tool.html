Migration to the United States

The United States is a top destination for immigrants seeking better economic opportunities, education, and political stability.

Core Documentation Tracking Milestones:

  • Visa Application: Submit through the U.S. Department of State's official website or USCIS for certain visa types
  • Background Checks: Fingerprinting and database checks for all applicants
  • Medical Examination: Required examination by a designated civil surgeon
  • Interview: Mandatory interview at a U.S. Embassy or Consulate

Migration to Canada

Canada is known for its welcoming immigration policies, offering numerous pathways for permanent residence and temporary stays.

Core Documentation Tracking Milestones:

  • Expression of Interest (EOI): Submit initial profile highlighting skills and experience
  • Invitation to Apply (ITA): Complete permanent residence application upon receiving ITA
  • Police Certificates: Required from all countries lived in for 6+ months since age 18
  • Medical Examination: Required exam by IRCC-approved panel physician

Migration to Australia

Australia attracts immigrants with its high standard of living, diverse culture, and strong economy.

Core Documentation Tracking Milestones:

  • SkillSelect Expression of Interest (EOI): Points-based assessment for skilled migration
  • Nomination/Sponsorship: May require state/territory nomination or family sponsorship
  • Police Certificates: Required from all countries lived in for 12+ months in past 10 years
  • Health Examinations: Medical exam by Australian Government-approved doctor
