Immigration to the United States

The United States is a top destination for immigrants due to its economic opportunities, educational institutions, and cultural diversity.

Key Search Pathways:

  • Work Visas (H1B, L1, etc.): Highly skilled workers often search for information on how to obtain work visas to enter the U.S. workforce.
  • Family Sponsorship: U.S. citizens and permanent residents searching for ways to sponsor family members to immigrate to the U.S.
  • Study Visas (F1, J1, etc.): International students looking to pursue higher education in the U.S.

Core Documentation Tracking Milestones:

  1. Application submission (e.g., DS-160 for non-immigrant visas)
  2. Biometric data collection
  3. Interview scheduling at a U.S. embassy or consulate
  4. Approval and visa issuance
  5. Entry into the United States and compliance with visa terms

Immigration to Canada

Canada is known for its welcoming immigration policies, diverse culture, and high standard of living, making it a highly sought-after destination.

Key Search Pathways:

  • Express Entry: Skilled workers seeking permanent residency through the Express Entry system.
  • Study Permits: International students interested in studying in Canada.
  • Work Permits: Individuals looking for temporary work opportunities in Canada.

Core Documentation Tracking Milestones:

  1. Creating an online profile and submitting an Express Entry profile
  2. Receiving an Invitation to Apply (ITA) for permanent residence
  3. Applying for a study or work permit
  4. Biometric data collection
  5. Approval and permit issuance
  6. Entry into Canada and maintaining status

Immigration to Australia

Australia offers a unique blend of urban and outdoor lifestyles, attracting immigrants with its quality of life and job opportunities.

Key Search Pathways:

  • Skilled Migration: Professionals looking to migrate through the General Skilled Migration program.
  • Student Visas: Individuals wanting to study in Australia.
  • Work and Holiday Visas: Young travelers seeking temporary work and travel experiences.

Core Documentation Tracking Milestones:

  1. Skills assessment by a relevant assessing authority
  2. Submission of an Expression of Interest (EOI) through SkillSelect
  3. Receiving an invitation to apply for a visa
  4. Applying for a student or work visa
  5. Health and character checks
  6. Visa approval and entry into Australia
