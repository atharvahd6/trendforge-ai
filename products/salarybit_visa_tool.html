Immigration to the United States

  • Work Visas (H-1B, L-1, O-1):
    • Petition for a Nonimmigrant Worker (Form I-129)
    • Labor Condition Application (LCA)
    • Valid passport
  • Family Sponsorship:
    • Petition for Alien Relative (Form I-130)
    • Application for Immigrant Visa and Alien Registration (Form DS-260)
    • Supporting documents (birth and marriage certificates)
  • Diversity Visa Lottery:
    • Application through the official DV lottery website
    • Further documentation (valid passport, birth certificate, police certificates) if selected

Immigration to Canada

  • Express Entry:
    • Online profile through the Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) website
    • Language test results (e.g., IELTS, CELPIP)
    • Educational Credential Assessment (ECA)
    • Proof of work experience
  • Provincial Nominee Programs (PNPs):
    • Application to the specific province
    • Police certificates
    • Medical examination
    • Proof of language proficiency and education
  • Family Sponsorship:
    • Application to sponsor a family member
    • Proof of income (Notice of Assessment)
    • Police certificates for the sponsored individual

Immigration to Australia

  • Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189):
    • Expression of Interest (EOI) through SkillSelect
    • Skills assessment by the relevant assessing authority
    • English language test results (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL)
    • Police certificates
  • Skilled Nominated Visa (Subclass 190):
    • Nomination by an Australian state or territory government
    • Skills assessment
    • Language test results
    • Health insurance
  • Family Sponsorship:
    • Application by the Australian citizen or permanent resident sponsor
    • Proof of relationship (e.g., marriage certificate, birth certificate)
    • Character certificates for the sponsor
