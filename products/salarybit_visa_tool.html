Immigration to the United States

The United States is a top destination for immigrants due to its economic opportunities, educational institutions, and cultural diversity. The primary search pathways for U.S. immigration include:

Core Documentation

  • Passport
  • Visa application (DS-160 for non-immigrant visas)
  • Proof of financial support
  • Health insurance
  • Police certificate
  • Educational transcripts and diplomas (for work or study visas)

Immigration to Canada

Canada is known for its welcoming immigration policies, high standard of living, and diverse culture. Key search pathways for Canadian immigration are:

Core Documentation

  • Passport
  • Language test results (English or French)
  • Educational credential assessment
  • Proof of work experience
  • Police certificate
  • Medical exam results
  • Proof of funds

Immigration to Australia

Australia attracts immigrants with its strong economy, beautiful landscapes, and high quality of life. Primary search pathways for Australian immigration include:

Core Documentation

  • Passport
  • Visa application
  • English language proficiency test results
  • Qualifications and transcripts
  • Work experience documents
  • Police certificate
  • Health insurance
  • Proof of sufficient funds
