# 🚀 Automated Startup Launch Kit (2026-05-26)

## 💡 Micro-SaaS Business Concept
PRODUCT NAME: PixelPerfect Annotate
THE LIVE TREND/PROBLEM: Remote agile teams waste countless hours on vague, manually annotated visual bug reports and design feedback that lack pixel-level precision and contextual data.
WHAT IT DOES: A browser extension and desktop app that intelligently captures screen regions, auto-detects UI elements for pixel-accurate measurements (dimensions, spacing, colors), and instantly generates version-controlled, shareable feedback links with categorized issues for dev/design teams.
TARGET AUDIENCE: UI/UX Designers, Frontend Developers, QA Testers, and Product Managers in remote or hybrid agile software development environments.
